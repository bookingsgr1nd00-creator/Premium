How ordering works

No processor — payment is handled by Interac e‑Transfer.

1) Choose products

Open the catalog and click “Order by Email” on the item(s) you want.

2) Email your request

Your email app opens with a pre-filled message. Add quantity + shipping city/province.

3) Pay by Interac e‑Transfer

We reply with confirmation details and the exact reference to include with your transfer.

Tip: For larger orders, you can email one message listing multiple SKUs and quantities.
